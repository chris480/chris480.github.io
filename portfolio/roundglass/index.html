Problem

RoundGlass sought to create a scalable, high-performance social platform to foster wellbeing and community engagement. The challenge was to architect a flexible, user-friendly solution that could underpin public-facing apps, internal tools, and new site launches, while bridging the gap between UX and engineering and ensuring best-in-class Accessibility, SEO, and Quality.

Solution

I defined the architectural direction for the Angular platform, aligning technical and UX goals with business objectives. Leading and mentoring a team of over 10 front-end engineers, I championed improvements in Accessibility, SEO, Quality, UX, speed, and independence. My approach included:

  • Architecting a modular, scalable Angular front-end for rapid iteration and maintainability.
  • Implementing advanced performance optimizations, resulting in up to 80% speed improvements in onboarding, content feeds, and messaging.
  • Developing a comprehensive design system powering the social platform, consumer apps, and email tools.

Successes

Our efforts produced measurable impact across the organization:

  • Platform speed improved by up to 80% in critical use cases.
  • Accessibility and SEO met and exceeded industry standards.
  • Delivery times drastically reduced through improved cross-team collaboration.
  • Design system adoption powered multiple consumer apps and email systems.
  • New sites launched in days, not weeks, using the site builder.
  • Unified brand experience and increased team independence, teams launched projects with minimal engineering support.

Leadership & Learnings

I mentored and empowered over 10 front-end engineers, fostering a culture of independence and continuous improvement. Presenting outcomes and best practices at internal summits influenced design system adoption across other RoundGlass initiatives. Leading a large engineering team and acting as a liaison between UX and engineering was key to breaking down silos and unlocking innovation.

Balancing ambitious performance and accessibility goals with the need for platform flexibility was a core challenge. Modularizing the architecture and prioritizing optimizations in user-critical paths, while mentoring the engineering team in best practices, ensured success.

Conclusion

By leading and mentoring a large engineering team and driving improvements in Accessibility, SEO, Quality, UX, speed, and independence, I enabled RoundGlass to deliver a high-performance, scalable, and cohesive digital ecosystem. This work exemplifies my approach as a principal UX and engineering leader, bridging disciplines, driving measurable impact, and empowering teams to create meaningful user experiences.
