Problem

Startups within Actuate's portfolio face extreme constraints: limited resources, aggressive timelines, and existential risk. Unlike established firms, these teams must iterate and ship rapidly, sometimes pivoting within days or hours to avoid failure. Actuate's value-add is not just capital, but hands-on leadership to accelerate product delivery, optimize team performance, and create investor-ready narratives.

Solution

As Product Management Lead, I directed teams of designers and developers for multiple portfolio companies, setting product vision, roadmaps, and execution plans. As an Investment Partner, I sourced and led investments, applying product and technical diligence to identify and nurture high-potential startups. Drawing on my founder experience, I empathized with and guided teams through high-stakes pivots, crisis management, and market launches. I managed cross-functional teams to deliver apps, decks, and shippable code, ensuring products reached customers and investors on time.

Case Study: Drone Inspection Platform for Aircraft and Spacecraft

One standout portfolio company developed a drone-based inspection solution for aircraft and spacecraft, dramatically reducing turnaround times for safety checks and maintenance. By automating inspections, airlines and space operators could return vehicles to service faster, improving operational efficiency and safety.

Contribution

  • Managed a multidisciplinary team of designers and developers, establishing clear milestones and agile workflows
  • Defined MVP scope and prioritized features based on customer feedback and regulatory requirements
  • Ensured rapid iteration cycles, with shippable code and customer demos delivered on a weekly basis
  • Navigated the team through resource crunches and technical blockers, maintaining morale and momentum during critical phases
  • Handled initial user research and customer discovery for many companies, leading to faster product market fit
  • Oversaw successful deployment to airline and aerospace clients, enabling measurable reductions in inspection times and downtime

Impact

  • Reduced aircraft inspection turnaround from days to hours
  • Enabled airlines and space operators to return vehicles to service faster, directly impacting revenue and safety
  • Maintained team focus and delivery under intense startup pressure
